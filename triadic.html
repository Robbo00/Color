Triadic Colors

What is Triadic?

Triadic color schemes use three colors evenly spaced on the color wheel, such as red, blue, and yellow. This creates a balanced and dynamic effect.

The vibrancy of triadic schemes makes them perfect for playful, bold designs or projects that require high energy.

Applications of Triadic Colors

  • Great for creating engaging and eye-catching designs.
  • Used in branding for a unique and vibrant identity.
  • Popular in digital media, advertisements, and graphic design.
